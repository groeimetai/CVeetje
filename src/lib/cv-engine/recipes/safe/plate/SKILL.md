# Plate — warm-neutral CV

A CV with the temperature of a quiet kitchen — warm bone paper, charcoal
ink with a hint of olive, a forest-green accent. Lato throughout, two
weights, restraint through space rather than stripping detail.

## Container

- A4 single column, 18mm margins.
- Warm bone paper (h=90).

## Header

- Name in Lato, 38pt, weight 900.
- Headline in Lato regular, 12.5pt.
- Contact line muted.

## Sections

- 'Clean' variant: small-caps title with hairline under.
- No kicker rule.

## Experience

- Bullets with green-accent dash markers.
- Period in small caps with tabular figures.

## Skills

- Pill tags with a soft accent tint.

## Anti-patterns

- Never use cool blues — this recipe lives in the green-warm-neutral band.
- Never use pure white paper.
- Never use a sidebar.
- Never use icons.
